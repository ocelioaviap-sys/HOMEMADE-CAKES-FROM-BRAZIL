Cake Types

Measurement Guide with Examples

Cup (240ml)

Use for measuring dry and liquid ingredients. Fill completely without packing, and level off with a knife.

Tablespoon (15ml)

Use for smaller quantities, such as baking powder or extracts. Fill and level with the back of a knife.

Kitchen Scale

For greater precision, weigh your ingredients. Measurements in grams are more accurate than volumes.
